About AirCrop

We combine innovative technology with deep agricultural expertise to provide efficient, sustainable solutions

AirCrop is a Drone-as-a-Service (DaaS) platform that uses AI to monitor crop health in real time. We help farmers detect plant diseases, pest infestations, and irrigation issues early—before they impact yields.

By combining aerial imaging and smart analytics, AirCrop empowers smarter farming decisions. Our AI-powered platform delivers actionable insights that save resources, reduce chemical usage, and maximize productivity.

Plant Health

Identify and understand crop diseases with precision. Our system captures high-resolution images and uses AI to detect signs of disease, pest damage, or stress.

Full analysis of crop health with disease classification, visual heatmaps and affected area segmentation, and actionable insights for treatment or prevention.

💧

Irrigation

AirCrop helps you monitor irrigation effectiveness across your field. From detecting dry spots to identifying overwatered areas, our platform helps you manage water more efficiently.

Detailed irrigation maps and moisture-level assessments, support for water-saving strategies and system adjustments, and improved crop resilience while reducing resource waste.
